About Me

View my resume here.

I am currently a junior attending a Southwest Career and Technical Academy, majoring in Web Design. I am an Adobe Certified Associate in Illustrator CS6, Flash CS6, and Photoshop CS6 and I am experienced in HTML5 and CSS.

I am an active member of the National Honor Society and currently the Vice President of the SWCTA SkillsUSA 2015-2016 chapter. I have competed in the Nevada State Competition and won silver for Promotional Bulletin Board.

I have been a member of SWCTA's award winning yearbook staff, The Howl, filling the role of Lead Graphic Designer since 2013 and have won a superior on one of my spread designs. I have also contributed to the online newspaper with the series "Sibling Rivalry," which I am no longer active in.

Photography is a side hobby of mine, and all my photos are taken on a Nikon D3200.
