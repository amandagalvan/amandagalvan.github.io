Hi there! I’m Amanda and I’m currently studying Graphic Design at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. I’ve had a passion for Web and Graphic Design since high school where I had the opportunity to become an Adobe Certified Associate in Illustrator, Flash, and Photoshop. Nowadays I regularly use InDesign and Premiere as well. At the moment I’m happy employed at Candy V Designs, a local creative agency ran by the best boss, but I continue to do freelance projects whether it be art or design!

When I’m not designing for school or work you can find me playing off-tank for my collegiate Overwatch team, Runnin’ Reinhardts. I'm a very laid-back person with a huge passion for esports, specifically the Overwatch League, and love to watch how the scene develops.

I suppose it’s also worth mentioning that I’ve competed in the 2016 ACA World Championships, placing 8th worldwide. It was definitely one of the best experiences of my life. For my portfolio, you’ll currently find projects I’ve completed from 2017 to the current day!
