Resumate.co

Your best mate when it comes to getting a job.

For our senior capstone project, I worked with Cynthia Kar. Together, we created a career preparatory website that worked as an all-in-one guide to getting a job. Resumate feautres three different interactive features: a resume builder, interview questions guide, and an outfit builder.

The resume builder is a step by step guide that allows you to create a resume you can print or save directly from the website using CSS for print. The interview questions guide features three different sets of questions that are applicable to any job that the user can fill in and print out to study before their interview. The final feature is the outfit guide, which shows what to wear to an interview at varying levels of professionalism. It also features what not to wear, all of which are useful things to know.

Resumate.co was created over the span of 9 months, crafted with careful planning and execution. This project was also brough to the Web and Game Senior Capstone Night at the InNEVation center and received the "Most Likely to Receive Venture Capital" award.
