About this Website


Me, working at the Candy V Designs old home office | Photo via @danniel.photography

This website was created as a high school project in my senior year, 2017, to show our understanding of code that we've learned throughout our 4 years. Everything is coded by me from scratch and fully responsive across different devices. While I don't create any hard-coded websites now (because of the many problems that creates in an ever-changing world of websites), I chose to keep my portfolio website hard-coded as a memory of my work and an example of my ability to code in HTML and CSS.

Currently when it comes to web design, I work almost exclusively in Wordpress with visual editors like WP Bakery and Elementor, as the company I work for provides these resources and so much more for our clients. I am a front-end designer, so I create wireframes, mockups and reproduce the designs on Wordpress for almost all of the clients we receive at my job.

Although this is an old website, I keep it updated with my most current pieces of work and still use it as a portfolio to this day. I believe this handmade website shows my passion and how much I believe in my work, and hope you can see the same!
